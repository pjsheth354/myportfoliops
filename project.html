CROP HEALTH ANALYZER

The Crop Health Analyzer is an instrument for determining the crop fertility or productivity.
It detects the amount of moisture and humidity in the crop and compensates it.
Crop Health Analyzer determines the amount of carbohydrates, proteins, vitamins and minerals.
It also focuses on techniques improving productivity and fertility of crops by means of fertilizers and pesticides.
